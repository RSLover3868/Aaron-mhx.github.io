hello grammar

wh-questions:  
    e.g.,Whose school bag is this?
pronouns:  
    mine,yous,his,hers,ours,theirs
imperatives:  
    e.g.,Put it on his desk,peter.
look and say: 
     Whose……?  It`s/They`er mine/yours/his/hers/ours/theirs.
think and write
    e.g.,The school bag is Sally`s.It`s hers.
    The_____is/are_____.It`s/They`er_____.
